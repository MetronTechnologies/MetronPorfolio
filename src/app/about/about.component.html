The job of a user interface / user experience designer is divided into two

User Interface Designer As a user interface designer, I have a human-first approach to product design
  • I apply my skills to digital products only
  • I focus on visual touchpoints that allow user's easy use of a product
  • I use a combination of typography, color palletes, buttons, animations and imagery
User Experience Designer As a user experience designer, I have a human-first approach to designing the aesthetic experience of a product
  • I apply my skills to digital and physical products
  • I focus on the full experience from a user's first contact to the last
  • I create structural design solutions for pain points that users encounter with using the product
As a frontend developer, I
